RSA for Encryption and Signature

Padding Schemes in RSA

RSA requires padding schemes to securely encrypt or sign data. Padding makes the data the correct size and adds randomness or structure to prevent attacks.

  • PKCS#1 v1.5 for Encryption- Deprecated (insecure if not careful)
  • RSAES-OAEP for Encryption - Recommended
  • PKCS#1 v1.5 for signature -Legacy, Still widely used
  • RSASSA-PSS for Signature - Modern standard
